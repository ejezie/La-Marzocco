la marzocco

For more than 90 years, we have been supplying premium coffee machines to the most coffee-passionate cafes and restaurants around the world.
To give people the opportunity to enjoy the highest grade of coffee at home and at work, we set out on a journey to develop a range of stylish and functional home machines that matches the quality of our commercial offering.
Our passion for coffee is what drives us to hand-assemble each of our iconic machines at our home in Florence, Italy, before delivering them to homes and workplaces that have coffee at their heart.
